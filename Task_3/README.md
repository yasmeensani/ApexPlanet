# Core KPI Definitions

The following KPIs were formally defined with formulas and business rationale.

---

## 1. Conversion Rate

### Formula
```text
(Total Conversions / Total Visitors) × 100
```

### Business Rationale
Measures the effectiveness of marketing campaigns, landing pages, or user journeys in converting visitors into customers or leads.

---

## 2. Customer Churn Rate

### Formula
```text
(Customers Lost During Period / Total Customers at Start of Period) × 100
```

### Business Rationale
Helps evaluate customer retention and identifies potential issues affecting customer loyalty.

---

## 3. Average Order Value (AOV)

### Formula
```text
Total Revenue / Number of Orders
```

### Business Rationale
Measures customer purchasing behavior and helps optimize pricing and sales strategies.

---

## 4. Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)

### Formula
```text
Average Purchase Value × Purchase Frequency × Customer Lifespan
```

### Business Rationale
Estimates the long-term value generated by a customer and supports retention-focused business strategies.

---

## 5. Retention Rate

### Formula
```text
((Customers at End of Period - New Customers) / Customers at Start of Period) × 100
```

### Business Rationale
Measures the company’s ability to retain customers over time.

# Selected Deep-Dive Area

## Cohort Analysis

### Objective
Analyze customer retention behavior and customer lifetime value across different acquisition periods.

### Analysis Performed
- Monthly customer cohorts created
- Retention trends analyzed over time
- Repeat purchase behavior evaluated
- Lifetime value patterns identified

### Key Insights
- High retention cohorts identified
- Customer drop-off periods detected
- Long-term customer value measured
