This repository showcases the complete journey of my Data Analyst Internship Program, demonstrating practical analytics skills applied to real-world business problems.

The portfolio is structured across four major phases of the analytics lifecycle:

1. **Data Immersion & Wrangling**
2. **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) & Business Intelligence**
3. **Deep-Dive Analysis & Interactive Dashboarding**

Each section contains project deliverables, insights, dashboards, reports, and business recommendations developed during the internship.
